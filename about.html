ABOUT US

Our Story

Founded in 2024 in Cape Town,South Africa, Whiskers and Wag Tails began with our founders passion for preparing healthy, homemade meals for her pets.This approach proved to be more affordable and nutritious than store-bought options.We have continued upgrading our formulation as well as business to keep our passion alive.

Our mission

To provide healthy, affordable pet food using natural ingredients and to help lengthen the relationship between people and their pets.

Our vision

To be the top pet food brand in South Africa, trusted by pet owners nationwide.
